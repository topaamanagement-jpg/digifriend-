What we look for
  • You must be 18 or older
  • Good spoken English (other languages a bonus)
  • Stable internet and a working camera
  • Reliable — you show up for your calls
  • Warm, curious, and easy to talk to
  • Available at least 2 hours per week
What this is not
  • Not a dating platform
  • Not therapy or emotional support work
  • Not adult content of any kind
  • Not a freelance marketplace or gig job
  • Not a platform for soliciting money or gifts

DigiFriend is for genuine, friendly conversation and cultural exchange. Applications that don't meet our standards will not be approved.

Community rules

By applying you agree to follow these rules at all times:

  • Never use the platform for dating or romantic purposes
  • Never send sexual, explicit, or inappropriate messages
  • Never ask members for money, gifts, or personal favours
  • Never share your phone number, WhatsApp, or external contacts
  • Never offer therapy, medical, legal, or financial advice
  • Never record calls without explicit consent
  • Always show up for your scheduled calls, or give at least 24 hours' notice
  • Always treat members with kindness and respect

Our team reviews every application manually. You'll hear back within 3–5 business days. We'll email you at the address you provided.
