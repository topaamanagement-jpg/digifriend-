About us

Why we built DigiFriend

Loneliness is one of the biggest health challenges of our time. We built DigiFriend to do something real about it.

Our story

DigiFriend started with a simple observation: millions of people around the world feel lonely, yet millions of others - in different countries, different time zones, different cultures - are warm, curious, and eager to connect.

We built a platform to bring these two groups together. Not through an algorithm trying to sell you something, but through real, recurring weekly conversations between two people who genuinely want to talk.

What we do

DigiFriend matches paying members with verified international conversation partners - people we call DigiFriends - for weekly video calls. Every DigiFriend is manually reviewed and approved before joining the platform.

The result is a simple, human experience: same person, same time, every week. Like having a friend in another country who actually shows up.

Our values

Real connection

We're not a networking app or a language exchange. We're here for genuine human warmth - the kind that comes from talking to the same person week after week.

Safety first

Every DigiFriend is verified before joining. We have clear guidelines and take violations seriously. Your comfort and safety come before everything else.

Global curiosity

We believe the world is a richer place when people from different backgrounds actually talk to each other - not just scroll past each other.

Simplicity

One call a week. One person. No complicated features. We keep it simple so the relationship can be the focus.

Get in touch

Questions, feedback, or just want to say hello? hello@digifriend.com - we read every message.

Real friendship, across borders.
