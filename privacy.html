Privacy Policy

We collect only what we need to run the platform. We never sell your data. Here's exactly what we do with your information.

Last updated: June 2026

1. What we collect

When you create an account or use DigiFriend, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your profile information (age, country, language preferences)
  • Booking history and call records (date, time, duration)
  • Payment information - processed securely by our payment provider, not stored by us
  • Communications you send through the platform

2. How we use your data

  • To match you with a compatible DigiFriend
  • To send booking confirmations and call reminders
  • To process your subscription and payments
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns

We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Who we share data with

We share limited data with trusted service providers who help us run the platform:

  • Supabase - our database and authentication provider
  • Resend - for sending transactional emails
  • Payment provider - for processing subscriptions
  • Jitsi Meet - the video call platform (no account required)

None of these providers are permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

4. Calls and recordings

DigiFriend does not record video calls. Calls take place on Jitsi Meet and are not monitored or stored by us. You should never record calls without the explicit consent of all participants.

5. Your rights

  • You can request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • You can request that we delete your account and data
  • You can update your profile information at any time
  • You can unsubscribe from emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@digifriend.com.

6. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required to keep you logged in and the platform functioning. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email hello@digifriend.com.
