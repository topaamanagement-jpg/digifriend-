For senior living & care communities

Bring the world into your community room

Group video calls with a DigiFriend from another part of the world - interactive, guided, and made for a room full of residents at once.

How it works

Instead of a single resident talking one-on-one, your community hosts a group call on a screen in a common room. Residents can ask questions, share stories, and hear directly from someone living in a different culture - guided by one of our verified DigiFriends.

🌍

A window to the world

Residents hear what daily life is really like in another country, straight from someone living it.

🗣️

Genuinely interactive

Not a lecture or a video - residents ask questions and the conversation goes wherever the group takes it.

🤝

Same care and screening

Every DigiFriend who hosts a community call is verified the same way as our one-on-one DigiFriends.

Pricing

There's no fixed monthly price for communities.

Group calls are billed per resident who joins the session - so a small activity room and a large common room both pay a fair price for what they actually use. We'll work out the details with you directly based on your community's size and how often you'd like to host calls.

Get in touch

Tell us a bit about your community and we'll reach out to set up a call and talk through pricing.
