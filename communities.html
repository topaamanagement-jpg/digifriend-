For senior living & care communities

Bring the world into your community room

On-site... virtually. Group video calls with a DigiFriend from another part of the world - hosted on a screen in your common room, open to as many residents as want to join.

Why communities host DigiFriend sessions

At DigiFriend, we bring genuine cross-cultural connection to senior living communities, assisted living residences, memory care units, and senior centers. Instead of one resident talking one-on-one, the whole room joins a live conversation with someone living a completely different life on another continent.

  • ✓A genuine window into daily life somewhere else in the world
  • ✓Real, unscripted conversation - residents ask the questions
  • ✓A shared activity that sparks conversation long after the call ends
  • ✓No travel, no special equipment - just a screen and an internet connection
  • ✓Every DigiFriend is verified and screened the same way as our one-on-one DigiFriends

Session formats

Every session is led by a DigiFriend and shaped around your residents - here are a few ways communities typically start.

A Day in My Life

A DigiFriend walks residents through an ordinary day where they live - food, family, work, weather - and takes questions as they come up.

Open Q&A

An informal, free-flowing conversation where residents ask whatever they're curious about - customs, history, current events, daily life.

Celebrations & Traditions

A look at a local holiday, dish, or custom - a nice fit for a themed activity day on the community calendar.

Recurring series

Some communities prefer a standing weekly or monthly session with the same DigiFriend, so residents build a relationship over time.

Pricing

There's no fixed monthly price for communities.

Group calls are billed per resident who joins the session - so a small activity room and a large common room both pay a fair price for what they actually use. We'll work out the details with you directly based on your community's size and how often you'd like to host calls.

Try a session with your community

Tell us a bit about your community and we'll reach out to set up an introductory call and talk through pricing. You can also reach us directly at hello@digifriend.app.
