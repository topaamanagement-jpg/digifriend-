How it works

Simple by design.

From signing up to your first conversation in under 48 hours. No complicated algorithm you never see. No endless swiping.

01

Tell us about yourself

Five minutes. Your interests, your timezone, what kind of conversations you're looking for. That's it.

02

Browse or be matched

We suggest someone right for you, or you pick one yourself from our verified list. Every person is manually approved before they appear.

03

Book a weekly time

Pick a slot that works for both of you. You'll get a reminder and a private video link before each call.

04

Show up and talk

Same person. Same time. Every week. That consistency is what turns a conversation into something that actually matters.

You're not paying for a video call.
You're paying for a person who shows up.

Safety & trust

DigiFriend is for conversation and cultural exchange. Not therapy. Not dating. Not a marketplace. Our rules are short, clear and strictly enforced.

  • ✓
    Every DigiFriend is manually approved - no one appears until our team has reviewed them.
  • ✓
    Private, time-limited video rooms - each call has a unique link that expires after the session.
  • ✓
    Zero tolerance for romantic content - violations result in immediate removal from the platform.
  • ✓
    Human moderation, not bots - every report is reviewed by a real person within 24 hours.
  • ✓
    18+ only - age is verified during signup and monitored by our team.
